Training & Placement Cell
The Training and Placement Cell functioning in the IIT Patna is entrusted with the responsibility of guiding students for placement in credible organization and arranging summer internship in reputed multinational companies and institutions in India and abroad. This cell also organise seminars, group discussions, and mock interviews as a part of career guidance and personality development.
